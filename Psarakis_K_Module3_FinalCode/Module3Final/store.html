How to Apply

What do I apply for?


You can apply to Ontario Colleges our Universities, programs at these institutions all vary in requirements to apply. It is crucial that you understand what is required of you before you begin your application.


Where do I apply?

Students can apply to college and universities in Ontario by using the links below.


eCampus Ontario: Click the link to Apply to Colleges in Ontario.


Contact North: Click the link to Apply to Universities in Ontario.


You will need to apply directly through a school if you are:


- Planning to study part time
-A mature student (generally of a certain age and away from formal education for some time)
-An international student
-Applying for graduate studies (e.g., a Masters or Doctoral degree)
-Planning to attend a private career college
